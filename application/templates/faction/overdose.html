Overdose Events

This table lists all overdoses of faction members. The drug overdosed upon is sometimes known when the member used the drug from the faction armory or if the member is signed into Tornium and is sharing this data. If the drug the member overdosed upon is known, it will be included; otherwise, it will be listed as "Unknown". As a result, when setting the overdosed drug filter, all overdoses with an unknown drug will not be included.
